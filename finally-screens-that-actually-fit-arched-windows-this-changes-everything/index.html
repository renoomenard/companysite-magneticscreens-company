Material Selection for Curved Applications

Installation Techniques for Curved Window Screens

Installing magnetic screens on arched windows requires specialized techniques that differ significantly from standard rectangular installations. The process involves careful positioning, gradual attachment, and continuous adjustment to ensure proper alignment along the curved perimeter.

The installation begins at the highest point of the arch, working systematically around the curve. Securing Magnetic Strips Along Curves

The key to successful curved screen installation lies in proper magnetic strip placement. These strips must maintain consistent contact with the window frame throughout the entire curve. This often requires strategic segmentation of the magnetic elements to ensure optimal adherence.

Dealing with Tight Curves

Sharp curves present additional challenges that require innovative solutions. The magnetic strips may need to be cut into smaller segments to follow tight radius curves effectively. This segmentation maintains the screen's integrity while ensuring complete coverage along the entire window perimeter.
