How Magnetic Window Mesh Works

The Science Behind Magnetic Attachment

Magnetic window mesh utilizes strategically placed magnets along the screen's perimeter. These magnets create a secure seal against metal window frames or magnetic strips attached to non-metal frames. It's like having invisible hands holding your screen perfectly in place, yet allowing easy removal when needed.
