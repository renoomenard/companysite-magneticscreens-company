Shipping & Returns

Last updated: May 2026

Delivery Coverage

Magnetic Screens Company delivers worldwide. We operate dedicated country stores for Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, Singapore, the United Kingdom, the United States, and many more countries globally. Orders placed through your local country store will be fulfilled from the nearest available dispatch location for the fastest possible delivery.

If your country does not have a dedicated local store, international delivery may still be available. Please contact us at theteam@customersupport.care for availability and delivery estimates for your location.

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary by country and chosen shipping method. The following are general estimates for major markets:

  • Australia: Typically 3–7 business days
  • United Kingdom: Typically 3–6 business days
  • United States: Typically 5–10 business days
  • Canada: Typically 5–10 business days
  • New Zealand: Typically 5–8 business days
  • Ireland: Typically 4–7 business days
  • Singapore: Typically 4–8 business days
  • Rest of World: Typically 7–21 business days

These are estimates only. Actual delivery times may be affected by customs processing, public holidays, carrier delays, and other factors outside our control. Estimated delivery windows will be displayed at checkout in each country store.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs vary by country and order size. Many of our country stores offer free shipping above a certain order value, and standard shipping rates apply below that threshold. The exact shipping cost for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout before you confirm your purchase.

We strive to keep shipping costs as low as possible and regularly review our carrier partnerships to ensure competitive rates for our customers.

Order Tracking

Where available, tracking information will be provided once your order has been dispatched. You will receive an email or notification from the relevant country store with your tracking number and a link to track your shipment with the carrier.

Customs and Import Duties

For international orders, customs duties, taxes or import fees may be assessed by your country's customs authority. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the product price or shipping cost. We recommend checking with your local customs office if you are unsure about potential import duties before placing an order.

Returns and Refunds

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or with a manufacturing defect, please contact us within 14 days of receipt with a description of the issue and supporting photographs. We will arrange a replacement or refund as appropriate, at no additional cost to you.

Returns for Change of Mind

Unused items in their original packaging may be eligible for return within 30 days of receipt, subject to the specific returns policy of the country store through which you made your purchase. The cost of return shipping for change-of-mind returns is generally borne by the customer. Items must be in original, unused condition with all packaging intact.

Custom-Sized Orders

Products made to custom specifications (non-standard sizes) are generally non-returnable unless they arrive damaged or defective. Please ensure your measurements are accurate before placing a custom order. Our team is happy to advise if you are unsure.

How to Request a Return

To initiate a return or report a problem with your order, please contact our customer support team:

  • Email: theteam@customersupport.care
  • Phone: +66 098 391 3877

Please include your order number, a description of the issue, and (for damage claims) clear photographs. We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days of the return being received and inspected. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider. You will be notified by email once your refund has been processed.
