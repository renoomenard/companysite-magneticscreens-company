Installation Best Practices for Contractor Grade Screens

Proper installation is crucial for optimal performance. Even the highest-quality screens from suppliers like the Best Danish Magnetic Screens Supplier or the Best European Magnetic Screens Supplier won't perform well if installed incorrectly.

Surface Preparation

Start with a clean, smooth surface around your window frame. Any dirt, paint chips, or debris can interfere with the magnetic seal, compromising the screen's effectiveness. Take time to thoroughly clean and prepare the mounting surface before installation.

Proper Measurement and Sizing

Accurate measurements are essential for a perfect fit. Measure your window opening carefully, accounting for any irregularities in the frame. Most professional suppliers provide detailed measuring guides to help ensure you order the right size.

Magnetic Strip Alignment

The magnetic strips must align perfectly for optimal performance. Take your time during installation to ensure the magnets create a consistent, gap-free seal around the entire perimeter of the screen.

Maintenance and Longevity

How do you keep your contractor-grade magnetic screens performing like new? Regular maintenance is surprisingly simple, yet incredibly important for maximizing your investment.

Routine Cleaning Procedures

Clean your screens regularly with mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals that might damage the mesh or magnetic strips. A soft brush can help remove stubborn dirt or debris without causing damage to the delicate mesh material.

If you live in an area with harsh winters, consider removing and storing your screens during the coldest months. This protects them from extreme weather and extends their lifespan significantly.
