The Science Behind Bug-Repelling Lights

Insects are naturally drawn to certain types of light, particularly those in the ultraviolet spectrum. Traditional incandescent bulbs emit light across a broad spectrum, including UV wavelengths that act like a neon "Welcome" sign for bugs. However, these innovative fairy lights use LED technology that minimizes UV emission while maximizing the warm, cozy wavelengths we humans love.
